A Few Accomplishments

Some of the things I am most proud of and some things that had a great impact.

Saved the metro of Paris

With my help, Siemens Mobility, in Paris, was able to offer a new tool for the preventive maintenance of the trains that operate in the metro of Paris.
My knowledge in embedded programming was key, and my ability to adapt to a completely new development environment and tools shined.

Revolutionized the automotive industry

It took all my wits, and hours of hard work to design and setup the best automated testing framework the dashboard industry had ever seen.
With the click of a button tests run, reports generate and schedules execute. Python was my weapon of choice.

Expanded the New Space frontiers

With my two feet in the ground I helped creating robust and genious systems that continue performing despite the harshness of the space environment.
Solar flares, cosmic rays, space debris. Nothing would stop our satellites from giving a different view of our planet and from delivering valuable messages through the waves.
